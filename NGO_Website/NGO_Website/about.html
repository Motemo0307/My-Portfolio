About Us

Daily Bread Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Tembisa, South Africa, dedicated to feeding the hungry and empowering vulnerable families through food security programs, nutrition education, and community support.

Our Mission

To fight hunger and restore hope by providing daily meals, food parcels, and nutrition awareness programs for children, families, and individuals in need.

Our Vision

A community where no one goes to bed hungry and every family has access to healthy, affordable, and nutritious meals.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Daily Bread Foundation began with a small soup kitchen serving a handful of families. Over time, with the support of sponsors and volunteers, it has grown into a well-recognized initiative that feeds hundreds of people weekly. Today, we continue to expand our programs to address the root causes of hunger and poverty.

Our Impact

  • Over 1,000 meals served every month.
  • Food parcels delivered to 200+ households in need.
  • Nutrition workshops reaching schools and families.
  • Partnerships with leading sponsors and supermarkets.

Meet Our Team

Our foundation is powered by passionate volunteers, dedicated community leaders, and caring sponsors who believe in making a difference, one meal at a time.
